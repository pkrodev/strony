FAQ

Most common questions before rollout.

Is it complicated for employees?

No. Signing takes a few clicks, and templates guide you step by step (fields + checklists).

Can I start with a single document?

Yes — we recommend starting with 1–3 most common processes and expanding later.

What does the customer receive after signing?

A PDF with signatures and an email confirmation (or a link). Everything is stored in history.

Can I collect multiple signatures on one document?

Yes. A document can go through multiple roles (e.g., employee → customer → manager). Every signature and status is visible in history.

Can I enforce a checklist and required fields?

Yes. A template can require completing fields (e.g., odometer, VIN, number of tires) and ticking a checklist — without that, the document can’t be finalized.

Does it work on phones and tablets without installing an app?

Yes — signing happens in the browser. You send a link, and the recipient signs on a phone, tablet or computer.

What about attachments: photos, notes, files?

You can add attachments (e.g., damage photos, confirmations, notes). This significantly reduces disputes about “who saw what”.

Can I search for a document by driver, vehicle or date?

Yes. Documents are stored in the archive and can be searched by field data (e.g., name, license plate, date, status).

Are there integrations with other systems?

Yes — integrations are possible via CSV export, API or webhooks (e.g., work orders, CRM, ERP). On the demo we define what data should sync automatically.

What does implementation look like and where is it best to start?

It’s best to start with 1–3 most common documents. We set templates, roles, checklists and run a short training — then we add more processes.

Can I use my own logo and branding?

Yes — depending on the implementation variant, we can prepare a branded view (logo, colors, document names).
