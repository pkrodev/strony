How FotoNaprawa works

A short, repeatable flow. Your team learns it in minutes.

  1. 1

    Select a job / vehicle

    Search by plate, VIN, or customer name. Or scan a code.

  2. 2

    Take “after repair” photos

    Tablet in hand, quick photo series. Add notes, parts, and checklist.

  3. 3

    Generate & send the report

    PDF or link with gallery + notes. Send via email/SMS.
