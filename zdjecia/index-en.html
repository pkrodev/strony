FAQ

Most common questions before implementation.

Does it replace my CRM/workshop system?

No — FotoNaprawa complements intake with photos and a protocol. It can integrate with your system to fetch work order numbers and customer data.

Is customer signature required?

No. Signature is optional and can be enabled if you want stronger confirmation.

Can I also take “after repair” photos?

Yes. The main focus is “before”, but you can also document at handover.

How does the customer receive the report?

As requested: PDF, link, or both. We set it up during implementation.

Does it work offline?

We can prepare a setup that doesn’t require constant internet in the workshop. Details depend on your integration/environment.

Where are photos stored?

Hybrid — on the workshop side with optional report sharing. The model is adjusted to your procedures.

Can I have my own checklists?

Yes. We tailor checklists and fields to your workshop type.

How long does implementation take?

Usually from a few hours to a few days — depending on integrations and number of stations.

Can you provide the tablet?

Yes. We can supply and configure the device.

Can I set roles/permissions?

Yes — e.g., only intake staff or only managers.

Can the protocol be numbered automatically?

Yes. With integration the protocol can be tied to the work order and vehicle automatically.

What support do you provide?

We offer service and adjustments after go‑live — scope depends on your needs.
