Features that keep the workshop in sync

Not “another system”. Just fast tools for work orders and customer communication.

Work orders + statuses

Create jobs, manage stages (received / in progress / parts / ready), keep deadlines.

  • Service checklists
  • Priorities
  • Today / tomorrow views

Customers & vehicles

One customer, many cars. History and recurring issues in seconds.

  • VIN / plate
  • Notes & consents
  • Document archive

Parts inventory

Stock, reservations per job, low-stock alerts, fast issuing.

  • Reservations
  • Low-stock alerts
  • Usage reports

Photos + customer report

Add before/after photos and generate a PDF or shareable link report.

  • Proof of work
  • Fewer disputes
  • More trust

Automated SMS/email

Notify customers: “parts ordered”, “ready for pickup”, “please collect”.

  • Templates
  • Delivery history
  • Status-based automations

Reports & KPIs

Revenue, repair time, returns, job count, bay utilization — without spreadsheets.

  • Dashboard
  • CSV export
  • Filters

  3. 03
