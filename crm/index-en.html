FAQ

If something isn’t in the default build, it’s usually a customization — ask us.

Is it a subscription?

No. WarsztatCRM is sold as a one‑time license. We configure it during deployment and provide lifetime service under agreed terms.

Does it work on phones and tablets?

Yes. It’s web-based and responsive.

Can I customize statuses and fields?

Yes. Statuses, fields, checklists and templates can be tailored to your workflow.

Can we keep data on‑premise?

Yes. We can deploy locally (on‑premise) or on your VPS/hosting.

Do you support SMS/email notifications?

Yes. We configure templates and automations (e.g., “ready for pickup”). SMS gateway depends on your needs.

Do you have parts inventory and reservations?

Yes. Stock levels, reservations per job and low-stock alerts are supported.

Can I attach photos and share a customer report?

Yes. Before/after photos can be attached to a work order and shared as PDF or a link.

Reports and exports?

Yes. Reports and exports (e.g., CSV) are available — we can tailor what you track.

Can you import data from another system?

Usually yes. We often start by importing customers/vehicles and extend from there.

How many users can use it?

Depends on deployment and licensing. We size it for your team and bays.

Can you build integrations?

Yes. Integrations (API, invoicing, other systems) are handled as extensions.

How long does deployment take?

A basic start can be done quickly (days). Workflow polishing happens iteratively.
