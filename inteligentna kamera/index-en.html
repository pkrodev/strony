Features that actually matter

We deliver software that recognizes entry/exit events and stores them in a local panel. We can tailor the system to your infrastructure and workflow.

Vehicle detection

The system detects a car in the frame and triggers analysis only when it makes sense.

  • fewer false events
  • cleaner logs
  • stable 24/7 operation

Plate detection

Finds the license plate region and prepares it for OCR.

  • different angles & distances
  • adaptation to mount position
  • calibration for local conditions

Plate OCR (ANPR/ALPR)

Reads characters and stores the plate number in history—no manual typing.

  • fast event search
  • entry snapshot preview
  • exports / reports on demand

Vehicle color detection

Speeds up filtering and verification, especially with higher traffic.

  • color as an extra attribute
  • easier history browsing
  • support for security checks

Optional: cameras & extra modules

If you want, we can pick the hardware and deliver a full deployment. As part of customization we can add additional attributes, integrations or automations (gate/barrier, internal systems).

FAQ

Most common questions before deployment.

Do I need internet connection?

No—typical setups work in your local network. Internet may be useful for remote maintenance, but it’s not required for recognition.

Where is the data stored?

On your side. The system is deployed on‑premise, and you define retention and what is stored.

Does it work at night?

Yes—depends on camera choice, IR/lighting and the mount position. We calibrate the setup for local conditions.

Do you provide cameras?

Optionally yes. We can work with your cameras or deliver a complete kit as part of the deployment.

Can it handle multiple cameras?

Yes. The exact scope depends on your setup and hardware. We size the deployment accordingly.

Can you customize the UI and reports?

Yes. Customization is a key advantage—we can adapt views, filters, fields and exports to your workflow.

Do you offer integrations (gate/barrier, API)?

Yes—as customization. Typical options: API/webhooks/file exports and automatic actions after plate recognition.

What exactly does “lifetime service” mean?

Support for the deployed system, fixes and updates as part of the cooperation—so the system stays stable and useful over time.

How do you price it?

Pricing depends on the number of cameras, mounting conditions, panel requirements and integrations. Share your context and we’ll be specific.

Can you add new features later?

Yes. We can expand the system with additional modules whenever you need them.

Is this a “camera” or “software”?

Primarily software deployed locally that works on camera video. Hardware (cameras) can be provided optionally.
